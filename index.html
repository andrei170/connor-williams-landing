FAQ

Common Questions

What if I don't get placed?

Full refund. No small print, no waiting period, no "we'll keep trying." If you complete training and I don't place you in a role earning $10K+/month, you get your money back.

Do I need sales experience?

No. A large portion of my students have zero sales background. Teachers, retail workers, service industry — they transition into closing successfully because the skills are communication-based.

Is this UK or US focused?

Both. Roughly 40% of my students are UK. We train for both UK and US sales culture and place into both markets.

How long until I'm placed?

Placement typically happens in weeks 5-8 after training. Full first commission month is usually months 3-4.

Who gets accepted?

Roughly 60% of applicants. I turn down people who don't seem coachable, who aren't ready to do the reps, or whose schedule won't allow the training commitment.
